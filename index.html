I am an Assistant Professor in computer science at the University of Wisconsin, Green Bay. Prior to this, I postdoc in the department of computer science and engineering (CSE) at the University of South Florida. As a member of the distributed systems research group (DSG) , I work in a transdisciplinary project titled "Modeling Information Diffusion Processes with Deep Learning Algorithms", funded by DARPA's Computational Simulation of Online Social Behavior SocialSim program.
Prior to joining USF, I worked at the University of Sydney, Central Queensland University and Federation University as an adjunct faculty. I also worked as a software and database developer before pursuing my PhD degree in computer science from the University of Sydney.

Research Interests

Network Science, Network Manipulation, Network security, Social Cybersecurity, and Human Computer Interaction
